Graduation

Dr. Sarah Benziane is a distinguished researcher and educator specializing in Artificial Intelligence with a robust academic background. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Sciences with honors from High School Cheikh Ibrahim Tazi in 1997. She then pursued an engineering degree in Computing, focusing on Parallel and Distributed Systems, at the University of Science and Technology of Oran, where she graduated in 2003 under the guidance of Dr. K. Kies. Continuing her academic journey at the same institution, she achieved a Magister in Electronics with honors in 2007, specializing in Vision and Pattern Recognition, and developed a mobile robot vision-based reactive navigation system under the mentorship of Prof. Ne. BERRACHED. In 2017, she completed her Ph.D. in Computing Science with a focus on Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence, with her dissertation supervised by Prof. A. BENYETTOU. Dr. Sarah Benziane has dedicated her career to advancing the field of AI through innovative research and committed teaching. She had her HDR in the same university in 2022.
